This process involved: Data Cleaning: Identifying and correcting inaccuracies, handling missing values, and standardizing data formats. Data Transformation: Structuring the data to enhance its usability for analytical and visualization purposes. The transformed dataset now provides a reliable foundation for insightful analyses and visualizations, offering valuable insights into the hospitality landscape of Lagos.
